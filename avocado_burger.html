Avocado Burger

Time Serves
30 Min. 2 Pers.

Ingredients

  • Pepper - 1 pinch
  • Salt - 1 pinch
  • Sesame seeds - 1 spoon
  • Olive oil - 1 fillet
  • Tomato - 1
  • Avocado - 2
  • Ground beef steaks - 2
  • Salas - 2-3 leaf
  • Red onion - 1/2

Usentils

  • Oven
  • Frying pan
  • Spoon
  • Dish

Steps

Step 1: Peel and cut the onion into rings.

Step 2: Wash and cut the tomato into slices.

Step 3: Cook the steaks in a pan with a drizzle of olive oil. Salt and pepper.

Step 4: Toast the sesame seeds.

Step 5: Open the avocados in half, remove the pit and peel them.

Step 6: Assemble the burger by placing an avocado half face up, place a steak, a slice of cheddar on the hot steak so that it melts, a slice of tomato, a slice of onion, a few salad leaves and finish with the second half of avocado.

Step 7 Sprinkle a few sesame seeds.
