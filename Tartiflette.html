Tartiflette

  • Time: 1 H
  • Serves: 8 Pers.
Ingredients Quantity
Potaotes 2,5 Kg
White wine 20 cl
Bacon 500 g
Salt 1 spoon
Pepper 1 spoon
Reblochons 2
Onions 7
Sour cream 30 cl

Step 1: Cook the potatoes in water for 20 minutes, then peel and slice them.

Step 2: Slice the onions and brown them in a little butter.

Step 3: Add the smoked bacon and also allow to return to fairly low heat (about ten minutes), stirring regularly.

Step 4: Prepare a baking dish (I am using a round terracotta dish 40 cm in diameter, 10 cm high). Generously rub the bottom of the dish with a clove of garlic.

Step 5: Organize the dish in three layers: cover the bottom of the dish with half the potatoes, then add the onion and bacon mixture, finally add the rest of the potatoes on top.

Step 6: Pour over the white wine and the whipping cream. Season with pepper (quite copiously) and salt (lightly).

Step 7 Preheat the oven to 190 Â° C (thermostat 6-7).

Step 8: Scrape the reblochons, cut them in half crosswise and place them (crust side up) on top of the potatoes.

Step 9: Bake for about 20 minutes (I put the oven on "rotating heat" + grill). When ready to serve (in the baking dish), sprinkle with a little chopped parsley. This unique dish is eaten (that's the right word!) Accompanied by the wine used for the recipe.
