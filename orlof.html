Orloff roasted veal

  • Time: 1H10.
  • Serves: 6 Pers.
Ingredients Quantity
Tomatoes 10 slice
Pepper 1 little spoon
Salt 1 little spoon
Roast Veal 1 Kg
Bacon 10 slice
Cheddar 10 Slice
Butter 50 g

Step 1: Preheat the oven at 180Â°C.

Step 2: Pre slice the roast (around 2 cm deep)

Step 3: Put 1 slice of cheddar, 1 slice of tomato and 1 slice of bacon between each pre-slice made in the previous step.

Step 4: Put the roast in a buttered dish and add some butter on the top of the roast.

Step 5: Salt , pepper.

Step 6: Let's put it on the oven for 1 hour.
