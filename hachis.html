Hachis Parmentier

Time Serves
45 Min. 4 Pers.

Ingredients

  • Worcester sauce - 1/4 drops
  • Mash potatoes - 300 g
  • Garlic - 2 pods
  • Tomatoes - 2
  • Flour - 1 spoon
  • Provencal grass - 1 little spoon
  • Parmesan - 30 g
  • Salt - 1 little spoon
  • Pepper - 1 little spoon
  • Chopped meat - 400 g
  • Onion - 2
  • Egg yolk - 1
  • Butter - 30 g
  • Rapped cheese - 50 g

Usentils

  • Oven
  • Frying pan
  • Spoon
  • Dish

Steps

Step 1: Choppe the garlic and the onion and make them come back in butter

Step 2: Add the tomatoes sliced in cube, the meat, the flour, salt, pepper and provencal grass.

Step 3: When all is cooked stop the fire and add the egg yolk and parmesan.

Step 4: Preheat the oven to 200Â°C.

Step 5: Spread out the meat in the dish and cover it with the mashed potatoes.

Step 6: Put it in the oven for 20 mins.
