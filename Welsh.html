Welsh

Time Serves
10 Min. 1 Pers.

Ingredients

  • Worcester sauce - 1/4 drops
  • Mustard - 1 spoon
  • Cheddar - 250 g
  • Bread - 1 slice
  • Ham - 1 slice
  • Egg - 1
  • Beer - 8 cl

Usentils

  • Oven
  • Frying pan
  • Spoon
  • Dish

Steps

Step 1: Put the mustard on the bread then brown it in the oven.

Step 2: Rap the Cheddar.

Step 3: Heat a frying pan with the beer over medium heat (thermostat 3-4 maximum).

Step 4: Once the beer is hot, and having evaporated some of the alcohol, add the grated cheddar in several batches, stirring continuously.

Step 5: Place the slice of bread au gratin with mustard in a ramekin-type dish, and place the ham on it.

Step 6: Once the cheese has melted, pour it over the toast and the ham (the mixture must be homogeneous, if the cheese is rubbery and liquid, the pan was too hot).

Step 7 Put in the oven for 2 to 3 minutes maximum to brown lightly.

Step 8: Fry the egg and place it on the Welsh once out of the oven.

Step 9: You just have to taste! You can add a little Worcester to the cheese as a seasoning (you can add the Tabasco directly to the cheese during cooking if you like).
